Sunday School

St. Thomas Marthoma Sunday School started in the year 1983 during the period of Vicar Rev.E.J Joseph. It remains the primary focus of the church for bringing up the children in discipline of the Lord from their childhood. Parish is proud of its Sunday school teachers and well-wishers for guiding and leading the children and arranging various programmes from time to time for the imbibing the Word of God in the hearts and minds of the children in the form of VBS, Camps, Inter Parish Competitions and seminars. We remember with gratitude the contribution of our past Sunday school teachers and mentors in moulding up a dedicated group of youngsters for the present and future.


“Train up a child in the way he should go and when he is old, he will not depart from it” Proverbs 22:6. May the Lord enable us to bring up our children in the fear of the Lord and an inspiration to be pillars of our church and community. Our Sunday School has nearly 40 students and 15 teachers
